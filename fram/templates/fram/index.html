Exploring the «inaccessible» part of the Artic Ocean



Fram-2019, a 5 month ice drift, will explore the submarine structures north of Greenland related to the late Cretaceous- early Teriary opening of the Eurasia Basin.

Responsible institution: Science: Department of Earth Science, Univ of Bergen
Field operation: Y-GEO, Bergen
Principal Investigators: Prof. Tor Arne Johansen & Prof. Emeritus Yngve Kristoffersen
Sponsors: Lundin Norge AS
